With over 100,000 users of Paypal and Skrill in Kenya, no one should have to wait over a week for the local banks to clear their dollars. Additionally, top up your PayPal and Skrill dollars by sending us the equivalent Kenya Shillings via M-Pesa. We are on a mission to get your funds to you faster and cheaper!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your current exchange rate?
Our exchange rate is updated hourly and displayed prominently on the website.
What if I have Kenya Shillings yet I need U.S. dollars?
Via M-Pesa, send us the shillings to convert at the rate of Kshs. 89.00. Then we immediately deliver the equivalent dollars to your Paypal or Skrill account.
How long does it take to receive my money?
Our transactions are completed in less than 10 minutes. In a few instances, we might have delays due to unforeseen technical issues with our mobile money partners.
How much is this really costing me?
The only fee we pass on to you is the tiered mobile operator's charge for sending money. For example, to send 1,500/-, we would deduct 33/-
How does the recipient know they have received their funds?
The recipient will receive a text message as soon as the funds are delivered.
What happens if the funds are delivered to the wrong phone number?
Okolea delivers money to the recipient mobile wallet instantly. If you provide wrong information, the transfer cannot be reversed or re-directed. Please check and confirm the number before making a transfer.
